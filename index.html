THE NEW RULES

MAG TOWN CODE

Built on principles. Run by standards. Loved by residents.

01

Security First

Every entry and exit is monitored 24/7. No unregistered vehicles enter without prior verification from security.

02

No Commercial Disruption

Residential zones are strictly residential. No shops, factories, or warehouses in living areas.

03

Clean & Green

Every plot owner maintains their frontage. Waste disposal follows society guidelines. No dumping of debris.

04

Construction Standards

Setbacks, height limits (max 3 storeys), and boundary rules must be followed for a uniform community look.

05

Monthly Maintenance

Nominal fee covers security, streetlights, garbage collection, park upkeep, and sewage management.

06

Community Respect

No loud music after 10 PM. Pets on leashes. No street parking overnight. Neighbourhood first, always.
