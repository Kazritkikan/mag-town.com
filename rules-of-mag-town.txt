              NEW RULES OF MAG TOWN
              Sialkot's Premium Housing Society
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════

These are the official rules and regulations for all 
residents and plot owners of Mag Town, Sialkot.

───────────────────────────────────────────────────────
1.  SECURITY FIRST
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────
    • All entries and exits are monitored 24/7 via CCTV.
    • No unregistered vehicles may enter without prior 
      verification from the security office.
    • Every visitor must be logged at the main gate.
    • Security personnel have the authority to inspect 
      any vehicle entering or leaving the premises.

───────────────────────────────────────────────────────
2.  NO COMMERCIAL DISRUPTION
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────
    • Residential zones are strictly residential.
    • No shops, factories, warehouses, or commercial 
      activities are permitted in residential blocks.
    • Home-based businesses that do not disturb 
      neighbours (e.g. freelancing, online work) are 
      allowed but must not involve customer footfall.

───────────────────────────────────────────────────────
3.  CLEAN & GREEN
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────
    • Every plot owner must maintain their frontage 
      and surrounding area.
    • Waste must be bagged and placed at the designated 
      collection point on scheduled days.
    • Dumping of construction debris on streets or 
      vacant plots is strictly prohibited.
    • Trees and green belts must not be damaged or removed.

───────────────────────────────────────────────────────
4.  CONSTRUCTION STANDARDS
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────
    • All construction must follow the approved building 
      plan from the Mag Town management.
    • Setback requirements: 5 ft front, 3 ft sides, 
      5 ft rear (for standard residential plots).
    • Maximum height: 3 storeys (40 ft from ground level).
    • Boundary walls must be uniform in design and 
      height (6 ft max).
    • No construction without prior approval from the 
      society's building committee.

───────────────────────────────────────────────────────
5.  MONTHLY MAINTENANCE
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────
    • A monthly maintenance fee is mandatory for all 
      plot owners and residents.
    • Fees cover: security personnel, CCTV maintenance, 
      street lighting, garbage collection, park upkeep, 
      and sewage management.
    • Late payment beyond 30 days incurs a penalty of 
      2% per month.
    • Repeated non-payment may result in restricted 
      access to society amenities.

───────────────────────────────────────────────────────
6.  COMMUNITY RESPECT
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────
    • No loud music, events, or noise after 10:00 PM.
    • Pets must be kept on leashes when outside the 
      home. Owners must clean up after their pets.
    • Parking is only permitted within your own plot 
      or designated visitor parking areas.
    • No vehicles may be parked on the streets overnight.
    • All residents are expected to treat neighbours 
      with courtesy and respect.

───────────────────────────────────────────────────────
7.  AMENITIES USAGE
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────
    • Parks and playgrounds are for all residents. 
      Timings: 5:00 AM – 10:00 PM.
    • The community mosque is open for all five daily 
      prayers and Jumu'ah.
    • Any damage to society property (lights, benches, 
      signs, etc.) will be charged to the responsible 
      party.

───────────────────────────────────────────────────────
8.  VEHICLES & TRAFFIC
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────
    • Speed limit within the society: 20 km/h.
    • Heavy vehicles (trucks, tractors, loaders) are 
      prohibited from entering residential streets.
    • Motorcycles must use designated parking areas.
    • Driving under the influence is strictly forbidden.

───────────────────────────────────────────────────────
9.  RENTAL & SUBLETTING
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────
    • Tenants must be registered with the society 
      management within 7 days of moving in.
    • Subletting of plots or portions thereof is not 
      permitted without written approval.
    • The plot owner remains responsible for all 
      actions of their tenants.

───────────────────────────────────────────────────────
10. AMENDMENTS
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────
    • These rules may be amended by the Mag Town 
      management committee with a 30-day notice to 
      all residents.
    • Suggestions from residents are welcome and will 
      be reviewed at the quarterly committee meeting.

───────────────────────────────────────────────────────
    For questions or reporting violations, contact:
    📞 Mag Town Management Office
    📍 Near China Chowk, Sialkot Cantt
